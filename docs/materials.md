# Materials

There will be different materials with different properties:

- Sand
- Stone
- Water

## Sand

Sand is sand-coloured (yellowish) and falls down on default.
When much Sand falls down it creates like a pyramid of sand so it falls down on the sides.

To be exact: When Sand has Air in the bottom right or bottom left then it will move there.

## Stone

Stone is gray and is fixed in the air.
So it doesnt fall down or anything it is just fixed.

## Water

Water is blue (who guessed it).
Water will be as much on the floor as possible so if the water can flow down on the same row it will move there
